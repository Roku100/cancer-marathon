About

Join the Race, Fight Cancer.

Be part of the movement to raise awareness and support for cancer care. Register now and make a difference!

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our Goal

Our Mission: To unite communities in the fight against cancer by raising awareness, supporting cancer patients, and funding cancer research and treatment through an annual marathon event. We strive to inspire hope, promote healthy living, and make a tangible impact in the battle against cancer.

Our Vision: A world where cancer is no longer a life-threatening disease, where every individual has access to early detection, quality care, and support. We envision a future where our marathon inspires lasting change and hope for cancer patients and their families.

Our Goal: To mobilize thousands of participants and supporters each year, raise significant funds for cancer care and research, and foster a supportive community dedicated to making a difference in the fight against cancer.

Our Goal

Together, We Run to Beat Cancer

Raise Cancer Awareness

Educate the public about cancer prevention, early detection, and the importance of regular screenings through community engagement and marathon participation.

Support Cancer Patients

Provide hope and financial support to cancer patients and their families by raising funds for treatment, care, and support services.

Promote Healthy Living

Encourage healthy lifestyles and physical activity as a means of cancer prevention and overall well-being for all participants and the community.

Why Support the Marathon?

Making Strides Against Cancer

The KUTRRH Cancer Marathon is more than a race—it's a movement. Every step you take helps raise awareness, funds vital research, and supports patients and families affected by cancer. Join us in making a real difference in the fight against cancer.

Raising Awareness

We shine a light on all cancer types, educating the public about prevention, early detection, and the importance of regular screenings.

Supporting Patients

Funds raised go directly to supporting cancer patients and their families, providing hope and essential care during their journey.

Funding Research

Your participation helps fund groundbreaking cancer research, bringing us closer to better treatments and a cure.

Building Community

We unite survivors, families, and supporters to create a strong, compassionate community dedicated to conquering cancer together.
